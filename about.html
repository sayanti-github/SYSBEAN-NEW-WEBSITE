About Sysbean

Sysbean is a strategic IT firm, primarily focused on Educational platform and multiple industry segments as well. We provide technology by means of a unique IT-as-a-Service model, offering end-to-end business solutions. It caters to the needs of multiple industry segments, through innovative, easy-to-use, secured, integrated, hosted solutions in a build-as-you-grow, pay-as-you-use business model. We serve our clients with the help of best practices gained through work experience, domestic market reach, skills, and delivery capabilities.

Cloud Based Solution is highly modular, scalable and configurable giving businesses and educational institutions the benefits of increased efficiencies, faster go to market, predictability of technology as well as spend and better business results.

In 2019, three engineers started Sysbean India with just their regular monthly income from their pockets. From the beginning, the company was founded on the principle of building and implementing great ideas that drive progress for clients in the educational platform and enhance lives through enterprise solutions. We recognize that Educational Institutes really need the change to automate all the necessary administrative and academic back-end processes. Meanwhile, we , at Sysbean India, analyzed that much needed change and became stubborn to provide a digital platform to this sector . We made 'CAMPUSIVE' as our very first Brand.
